3. Add clears for SM

2 column layout at SM needs a clear every second block.

[1] block sm-c6 clear-all
[2] block sm-c6

4. Change behaviour for MD

Keep the behaviour classes together to make it easier to understand.

[1] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-all
[2] block sm-c6 md-c4
[3] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm
[4] block sm-c6 md-c4
[5] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm
[6] block sm-c6 md-c4
[7] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm
[8] block sm-c6 md-c4

5. Add clears for MD

3 column layout at MD needs a clear every third block.

[1] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-all
[2] block sm-c6 md-c4
[3] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm
[4] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-md
[5] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm
[6] block sm-c6 md-c4
[7] block sm-c6 md-c4 clear-sm clear-md
[8] block sm-c6 md-c4

6. Change behaviour for LG

Again, keep the behaviour classes together to make it easier to understand.

[1] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-all
[2] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3
[3] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm
[4] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-md
[5] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm
[6] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3
[7] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm clear-md
[8] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3

7. Add clears for LG (and wider)

4 column layout at LG needs a clear every fourth block. Note the "-up" suffix to apply this clear at wider layout breaks as well.

[1] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-all
[2] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3
[3] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm
[4] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-md
[5] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm clear-lg-up
[6] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3
[7] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3 clear-sm clear-md
[8] block sm-c6 md-c4 lg-c3
